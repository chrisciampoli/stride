Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Stride ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Stride mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo (optional).

Health & Fitness Data: With your explicit permission, we access the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Step count
  • Walking/running distance
  • Active energy burned (calories)
  • Exercise minutes

User-Generated Content: Messages you send to other users, challenge participation data, and profile information you choose to share.

Device Information: We collect push notification tokens to deliver notifications to your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Stride app functionality
  • To display your fitness data in challenges and leaderboards
  • To send push notifications about challenges, friends, and achievements
  • To calculate streaks and award badges based on your activity

3. Apple HealthKit Data

We comply with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

  • HealthKit data is not stored in iCloud or any third-party cloud service beyond our secure database
  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • HealthKit data is not shared with third parties
  • HealthKit data is not sold under any circumstances
  • HealthKit data is only used to power the core features of the app (challenges, leaderboards, streaks)

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) policies. This means you can only access your own data and data explicitly shared with you through friendships and challenges. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

5. Data Sharing

We share limited data with other Stride users as part of the app's social features:

  • Your profile name, avatar, and bio are visible to your friends
  • Your step counts are visible to participants in your challenges
  • Messages you send are visible to the recipient

We do not sell your data to third parties.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app (Profile → Delete Account). This permanently removes:

  • Your profile and avatar
  • All challenge participation and step history
  • All messages, friendships, and notifications
  • All badges and achievements
  • Your authentication account

7. Children's Privacy

Stride is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@strideapp.com.
